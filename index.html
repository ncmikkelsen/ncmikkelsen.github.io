About

Hi!

I'm an interaction designer currently doing my master's degree in Digital Design at Aarhus University. I create interactive installations and experiments with processing and arduino. I also dabble in javascript and python.

My work has been exhibited at Silkeborg Bad Kunstmuseum, Media Architecture Biennale 2014, Aarhus Psych Fest 2015, ACM: Creativity and Cognition 2015 and Aarhus Festuge 2016.

I also teach and help students create technical prototypes at the Digital Design Lab at Aarhus University.
